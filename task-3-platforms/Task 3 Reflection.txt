Task 3 Reflection: Coding & Collaboration Platforms

For Task 3, I explored coding and collaboration platforms by using HackerRank
and Google Workspace. On HackerRank, I completed the beginner-level 'Solve Me
First' Python challenge. This allowed me to practice basic syntax and understand
how automated testing environments evaluate outputs. For the collaboration
aspect, I used Google Forms to build a 5-question 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz'
covering topics like 2FA and professional email etiquette. I also linked the form to
a Google Sheet to automatically collect responses. These tools will be highly
beneficial academically to gather survey data for group presentations or track
project data efficiently.
