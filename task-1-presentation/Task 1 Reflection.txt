Task 1 Reflection: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

For the first task of this project, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic
using Canva. My design focuses on three critical areas for engineering students:
safe internet practices, professional online presence, and effective email etiquette.
I used a clean, professional template with icons to make the information easy to
scan for my batchmates. One thing I found particularly interesting while making it
was how many different elements go into "Digital Literacy" beyond just basic
computer skills—it’s really about how we conduct ourselves and stay safe in a
connected world. The most difficult part was condensing the information to fit on
a single page while keeping it visually appealing. This task helped me realize that
as a Student Digital Ambassador, visual communication is just as important as
technical knowledge
