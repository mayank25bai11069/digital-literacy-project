Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

5 DO's:

Do review your privacy settings regularly: Always know exactly who can see your posts, photos, and personal details.

Do think before you post: Remember your digital footprint; once something is online, it is almost impossible to completely erase.

Do network professionally: Use platforms like LinkedIn or Twitter to connect with people in your industry and learn from their content.

Do verify information before sharing: Always fact-check news articles or viral posts to avoid spreading misinformation.

Do maintain a respectful tone: Keep your comments and interactions polite, even if you disagree with someone.

5 DON'Ts:

Don't overshare personal information: Never post your home address, phone number, personal daily schedule, or financial details.

Don't engage in online arguments: Avoid getting dragged into toxic comment sections or trolling; it's not worth your time or digital reputation.

Don't post unprofessional content: Avoid sharing anything that you wouldn't want a future employer or internship coordinator to see.

Don't accept requests from strangers: Be cautious of fake accounts, bots, or people you don't know in real life.

Don't click on suspicious links: Even if it is sent by a friend's account, verify before clicking to protect yourself from phishing or malware.
