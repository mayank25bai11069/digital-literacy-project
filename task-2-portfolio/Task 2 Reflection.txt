Task 2 Reflection: Digital Portfolio

For my digital portfolio, I chose to set up profiles on GitHub, Stack Overflow, and
Kaggle. I selected GitHub because it is the industry standard for version control
and hosting code; over the next four years, I plan to use it to securely store my
college assignments and showcase the Python and C++ projects I am building. I
created a Stack Overflow account because it is the largest community for
developers, and I will rely on it heavily to troubleshoot syntax errors and figure
out complex logic. Finally, I chose Kaggle because it is a massive hub for datasets.
While I am just starting out, I plan to use Kaggle in the future to pull historical
datasets to analyze data trends. Together, these three platforms give me a solid
foundation.
